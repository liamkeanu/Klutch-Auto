The Founder

One rep. Two years on top.

Liam Aguiling spent two years as a sales associate at a Chrysler Dodge Jeep Ram dealership — and spent two of those years at the top of the board. Not by luck. Not by working more hours. By mastering a channel everyone else was sleeping on: Facebook Marketplace.

While other reps were copying and pasting listings one by one, Liam was engineering a system. While others were posting and forgetting, he was refreshing, reposting, and routing leads in real time. Marketplace wasn't a side channel — it was his primary source of floor traffic.

The tools didn't exist yet. So he built them.

Why Klutch Exists

Every rep has the same problem.
Nobody was solving it.

Marketplace is the single most underutilized lead source in automotive retail. The inventory is there. The buyers are there. But most dealerships are posting the same copy-paste listings as everyone else — and wondering why nobody calls.

The reps who close on Marketplace aren't posting more. They're posting smarter. They understand visibility windows, pricing signals, group distribution, and follow-up timing. That's the Klutch Method — the same playbook Liam ran for two years before anyone else knew it worked.

Klutch automates the mechanical work so you can focus on the strategy. Auto Lister pulls your inventory and fills every field. Group Poster distributes to the right audiences. Price Refresher keeps you visible when listings go stale. The method handles the rest.

The floor is talking — are you listening? Most reps are posting. Klutch reps are closing.
